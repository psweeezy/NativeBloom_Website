New native wildflower garden in Oakville's Coronation Park

In partnership with ONplants, Native Bloom is a native wildflower garden available for exploration and enjoyment in Corontation Park. Within the garden, six focal wildflowers will be on display, available to be collected in your own 'virtual garden' through the Native Bloom app. Collect all six plants to see your own colourful native garden bloom!
